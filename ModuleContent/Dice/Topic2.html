Understanding Single Die Outcomes

When working with a single die, the probability calculations are straightforward. Each face of a standard six-sided die has an equal probability of 1/6. This fundamental concept helps us understand basic probability theory.

Key Principles

  • Equal Probability: Each number (1-6) has probability 1/6 = 16.67%
  • Even Numbers: Probability of 2, 4, or 6 = 3/6 = 1/2 = 50%
  • Odd Numbers: Probability of 1, 3, or 5 = 3/6 = 1/2 = 50%
  • Specific Range: Probability of rolling 3 or 4 = 2/6 = 1/3

Complementary Events

  • P(rolling 6) = 1/6, therefore P(NOT rolling 6) = 1 - 1/6 = 5/6
  • P(rolling at least 4) = P(4, 5, or 6) = 3/6 = 1/2
  • P(rolling less than 3) = P(1 or 2) = 2/6 = 1/3

Practice Tip: Always verify probabilities add up to 1 when covering all possible outcomes!
