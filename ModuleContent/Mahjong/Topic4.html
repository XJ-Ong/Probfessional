Understanding Mahjong Tiles

A standard Mahjong set has 144 tiles:

  • 36 Bamboo tiles
  • 36 Character tiles
  • 36 Dot tiles
  • 12 Dragon tiles (4 each of 3 types)
  • 16 Wind tiles (4 each of 4 directions)
  • 8 Flower tiles

Basic Probabilities:

  • P(bamboo tile) = 36/144 = 1/4
  • P(dragon tile) = 12/144 = 1/12
  • P(flower tile) = 8/144 = 1/18
  • P(character tile) = 36/144 = 1/4
  • P(wind tile) = 16/144 = 1/9
