Mahjong Probability Strategies

Sequential Draws:

  • P(dragon then wind): (12/144) × (16/143) = 1/108
  • Order matters in sequential draws

Color/Type Probabilities:

  • If a tile is green, P(it''s green dragon): 4 green dragons / total green tiles
  • P(green dragon | green tile) = 4/144 = 1/36

Winning Combinations:

  • Mahjong involves creating specific tile combinations
  • Understanding tile probabilities helps predict opponents'' hands
  • Strategic play requires calculating remaining tile probabilities
