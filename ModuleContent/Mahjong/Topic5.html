Mahjong Tile Drawing

Mahjong involves drawing tiles without replacement, so probabilities change with each draw.

Multiple Draws:

  • P(both dots without replacement): First dot = 36/144, second dot = 35/143
  • P(both dots) = (36/144) × (35/143) = 35/572

Complement Rule:

  • P(not flower) = 136/144
  • P(first tile not flower) = 136/144

Specific Tiles:

  • There are 4 tiles of each numbered rank
  • P(drawing 1-bamboo in three draws): Complex calculation involving combinations
  • P ≈ 1/36

Practice:

  • P(both dots) = (36/144) × (35/143) = 35/572
  • P(not flower) = 136/144
  • P(1-bamboo in three draws) ≈ 1/36
