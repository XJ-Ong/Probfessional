Mahjong Tile Categories and Their Probabilities

Understanding the distribution of tile categories in Mahjong is essential for calculating probabilities accurately. Each category has different counts, affecting their individual probabilities.

Major Categories

  • Suit Tiles: Bamboo, Character, Dot (36 each = 108 total)
  • Each suit has probability 36/144 = 1/4 = 25%
  • Honor Tiles: Dragons (12) + Winds (16) = 28 total
  • Special Tiles: Flowers (8 tiles = 1/18 probability)

Calculating Specific Probabilities

  • P(specific suit, e.g., bamboo) = 36/144 = 1/4
  • P(dragon tile) = 12/144 = 1/12 ≈ 8.33%
  • P(wind tile) = 16/144 = 1/9 ≈ 11.11%
  • P(flower tile) = 8/144 = 1/18 ≈ 5.56%

Combined Probabilities

  • P(any suit tile) = 108/144 = 3/4 = 75%
  • P(honor or flower) = (28 + 8)/144 = 36/144 = 1/4 = 25%
  • P(NOT a flower) = 136/144 = 17/18 ≈ 94.44%

Remember: Probabilities must add up to 100% when considering all possible outcomes!
