Standard Poker Deck

A standard deck has 52 cards: 4 suits (hearts, diamonds, clubs, spades) with 13 ranks each (A, 2-10, J, Q, K).

Probability Basics:

  • Probability of drawing an Ace: 4/52 = 1/13
  • Probability of drawing a red card: 26/52 = 1/2
  • Probability of drawing a heart: 13/52 = 1/4
  • Probability of drawing a King: 4/52 = 1/13

Key Formula: P(Event) = Number of favorable outcomes / Total possible outcomes
