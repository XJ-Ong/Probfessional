"At Least One" Problems

Complement Rule: P(at least one) = 1 - P(none)

Examples:

  • If P(jackpot) = 1/250, play 3 times:
  • P(at least one jackpot) = 1 - P(no jackpots)
  • P(no jackpots) = (249/250)³
  • P(at least one) = 1 - (249/250)³ ≈ 0.01196

Multiple Failures:

  • P(diamond) = 1/16, play 10 times
  • P(never get diamond) = (15/16)^10
  • Use complement: P(at least one diamond) = 1 - (15/16)^10

Key Takeaway: Each spin is independent - past results don''t affect future probabilities!

Practice:

  • P(jackpot in 3 spins) = 1 - (249/250)³ ≈ 0.01196
  • P(never diamond in 10 spins) = (15/16)^10
  • P(at least one diamond) = 1 - (15/16)^10
