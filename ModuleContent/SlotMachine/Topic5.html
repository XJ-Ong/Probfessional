Multiple Spins and Symbol Combinations

Equal Probability Symbols: If there are 5 symbols with equal probability:

  • P(any symbol) = 1/5
  • P(three of same symbol) = (1/5)³ = 1/125

Multiple Spins:

  • P(bell twice in two spins): (1/20) × (1/20) = 1/400
  • P(cherry then orange): (1/10) × (1/10) = 1/100

Different Symbols:

  • With 8 symbols, P(three sevens): (1/8)³ = 1/512
  • P(bar, cherry, orange in any order): 6 permutations × (1/10)³ = 6/1000

Practice:

  • P(bell twice) = (1/20)² = 1/400
  • P(cherry then orange) = (1/10)² = 1/100
  • P(three sevens, 8 symbols) = (1/8)³ = 1/512
