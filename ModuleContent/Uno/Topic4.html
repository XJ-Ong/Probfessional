Understanding the UNO Deck

A standard UNO deck has 108 cards:

  • 4 colors (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow) × 25 cards each = 100 colored cards
  • 8 Wild cards
  • 8 special action cards (Skip, Reverse, Draw Two)

Basic Probabilities:

  • P(red card) = 25/108
  • P(wild card) = 8/108 = 2/27
  • P(skip card) = 8/108 = 2/27
  • P(blue reverse) = 2/108 = 1/54
