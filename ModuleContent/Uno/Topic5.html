Card Drawing Probability

When drawing cards without replacement, the deck size decreases, affecting probabilities.

Examples:

  • P(no red in 5 cards): Draw 5 cards, none are red
  • First card not red: 83/108
  • Second card not red: 82/107
  • Continuing: P = (83/108) × (82/107) × (81/106) × (80/105) × (79/104) ≈ 0.24

Sequential Events:

  • P(wild then skip): (8/108) × (8/107) ≈ 0.0055
  • P(first card wild, second skip): (8/108) × (8/107)

Conditional Probability: If all number cards are gone, P(drawing wild) = 8/32 = 1/4

Practice:

  • P(no red in 5 cards) ≈ 0.24
  • P(wild then skip) ≈ 0.0055
  • P(top card not a number card) = 32/108
